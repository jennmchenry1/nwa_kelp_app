Each layer shows the projected change in kelp species distributions between the present-day (2010-2020) and three SSP scenarios. 

Each is a classified maps showing stable presence (-1), stable absence (0), loss (-2), and gains (1) for each species.

Species codes are : Ac- Agarum clathratum, Ae- Alaria esculenta, Ld- Laminaria digitata, and Sl- Saccharina latissima
